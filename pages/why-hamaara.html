OUR PRINCIPLES · 01

Production first, always.

Every principle we hold comes from shipping real systems for real businesses and learning what breaks when the stakes are high.

  • 01
    Co-build, not throw-over

    We work inside your repo, your CI, your Slack. No external portal, no handoff doc, no baton pass.

  • 02
    Senior people on senior problems

    The engineer who scopes the work is the engineer who ships it. No bait-and-switch, no escalation chain.

  • 03
    Instruments before guesses

    We agree metrics upfront. ROI, cycle time, error budget — tracked weekly, shared openly.

  • 04
    Runbooks ship with the code

    Every system we build comes with the documentation to operate it. Ownership transfers cleanly.

  • 05
    Quarterly optionality

    Ramp up, ramp down, or take it in-house at any quarterly boundary. No lock-in.
